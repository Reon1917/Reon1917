Spice Rich Sand Dunes

Dunes on Northern Hemisphere

Picture taken in 10160s AG during the Harkonnena rule of Arrakis

Dune, it is everywhere on Arrakis and one of the most iconic scenes. It is rich with spice melange which drawn the attention of many Imperial Houses. Spice grants its user extended life, hightened senses which is the reason why Spacing Guild Navigators use it to calculate for space travel. It is also a home to dangerous sand worms (Shai-Hulud or The Maker by the Fremens) and many more fauna present on the planet


Sandworms

Sandworms are the predominant lifeforms of Arrakis feared and honored by Fremens

Picture of Sandworms taken during the Battle of Arakeen, 19193 AG November

Sandworms are creatures that can grow up to 450 meters in length and major danger for spice mining operations. However, they are also the producers of spice on Arrakis. Fremens have many names for it such as Shai-Hulud, The Maker or Grandfather of the desert. Younger, smaller sandworms are used by Fremens in order to produce "The Water of Life" for special ceremonies.
